Why Co.hit ?

I started Co.hit at a time in my life when I finally found myself.

I focused on myself, let go, stopped having expectations of others, and concentrated on a goal, the most important to me.
I started meditating again, reading books that interested me, delving deeper into spirituality.
I began drinking more water, adopting more decent sleep schedules, eating better.

It's when we change all our bad habits for the better ones that we realize how destructive they were, how much peace of mind and happiness are truly accessible to everyone. We all want to be well, but no one knows how. It requires discipline and courage, but it's worth it, hit that girl!

I will do my best to accompany you.
